Kobe Bryant

American professional basketball player.

Here's a time line of Kobe Bryant life:

  • 1978-Born in philadelphia
  • 1996-Selected 13th overall in the NBA draft by the Charlotte Hornets out of Lower Merion High in Pennsylvania.
  • 1997-Rookie Season,Bryant won the Slam Dunk Contest at NBA all-star weekend.
  • 1998-Made first of his 18 All-Star apperanc,Which included 17 staright from 2000-16.
  • 2000-Won his first of five NBA championships (2000-02,2009-10).That season, he also made the first of his nine All-NBA Defensive first teams.
  • 2002-Named to first of 11 All-NBA first Teams,Which is tied for the most in NBA history with karl and Lebron.
  • 2006-Scored 81 points in a game against the Toronto Raptors, Which is the second most points scored in the NBA history.
  • 2008-Won his lone NBA MVP award
  • 2009-Won his first NBA Finals MVP Award before winning it again in 2010.
  • 2016-In his final NBA game, Bryant put forth a vintage performance, scoring 60 points against the Jazz in a 101-96 Lakers win, becoming the oldest player in NBA history to score 60 in a game. That is also the most points any NBA player has ever scored in his final game. He finished his career with 33,643 points, which was third in NBA history until James surpassed the mark on Jan. 25, 2020. He had played his final game in Salt Lake City a few weeks prior, where the Jazz played a tribute video to honor him.
  • 2020-Died in a helicopter crash along with eight others, including his daughter Gianna, in California at the age of 41.
